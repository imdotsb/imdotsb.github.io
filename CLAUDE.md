## Design Context

### Users
This page serves a mixed audience: existing users who use it as a fast launchpad into im.sb services, and first-time visitors who need to quickly understand what the ecosystem offers.

Users are primarily trying to scan available services, understand what each tool does at a glance, and jump into the right destination with minimal friction.

### Brand Personality
The brand should feel calm, premium, and confident.

The current interface already suggests a restrained, polished directory experience rather than a loud or playful one. The emotional goal should be trust, clarity, and quiet quality.

### Aesthetic Direction
Favor a light-only interface with soft surfaces, clean typography, restrained color, and deliberate emphasis.

The existing design language points toward:
- airy spacing
- card-based organization
- subtle elevation and gradients
- concise, utility-first presentation

Avoid aesthetics that feel noisy, overly saturated, gamified, or aggressively “startup flashy.”

### Design Principles
1. Prioritize fast comprehension. Users should immediately understand what each service is and where to click.
2. Keep the tone calm and premium. Use restraint in color, motion, and decoration.
3. Preserve a light, polished visual system. Favor whitespace, soft contrast, clean type, and subtle depth.
4. Design for mixed familiarity. The page should work equally well as a launchpad for returning users and an introduction for new visitors.
5. Make utility feel refined. Functional elements should be simple and efficient, but never crude or visually careless.
